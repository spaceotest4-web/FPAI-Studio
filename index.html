Powerful AI Features

Everything you need to create professional AI-generated content, all in one app.

AI Image Generation

Generate stunning images from text prompts using 6 AI models including Mystic, Flux 2 Pro, Flux Dev, Seedream, and HyperFlux. Fine-tune with aspect ratio, guidance scale, and seed controls.

AI Video Generation

Create videos from text with 10 AI models. Supports Kling V3 Pro multi-shot scenes, Luma Dream Machine, Minimax, Wan-X, and more with duration, audio, and motion controls.

Kling Motion Control

Transfer motion from a reference video to any character image. Supports character orientation, CFG scale, and custom prompts for precise video-to-character motion transfer.

Image Editing

Upscale images to 4x, relight photos with AI, transfer artistic styles with reference images, and expand image canvas boundaries using Flux Pro AI expansion.

Stock Image Search

Search millions of high-quality stock photos, vectors, and illustrations from Freepik's library. Filter by content type for the perfect asset every time.

Multi-Shot Scenes

Create complex video narratives with up to 6 sequential shots. Each shot has its own prompt and duration for complete creative control over your AI-generated stories.
