AI Style Transfer: Transform Any Photo into Stunning Art

7 min read

AI Style Transfer Tutorial

What Is AI Style Transfer?

AI style transfer takes the visual style of one image — its colors, textures, brushstrokes, and artistic feel — and applies it to another image while preserving the original content. Imagine taking your vacation photo and rendering it in the style of Van Gogh's Starry Night, or turning a portrait into a watercolor painting.

Unlike simple filters that apply uniform effects, AI style transfer understands the structure of both images. It applies artistic techniques contextually — adding heavier brushstrokes to skies, finer detail to faces, and appropriate textures to different surfaces.

How to Use Style Transfer in FP AI Studio

  1. Open the Image Editing tab and select Style Transfer
  2. Enter the URL of your source image — the photo you want to transform
  3. Enter the URL of your reference image — the artwork whose style you want to apply
  4. Optionally add a prompt to guide the transformation (e.g., "make it more vibrant" or "keep the face realistic")
  5. Tap Process and wait for the result

Choosing the Right Reference Image

The reference image is the key to great style transfer. Here's what works well:

Paintings & Traditional Art

  • Impressionist paintings — Produce dreamy, light-filled results with visible brushwork
  • Oil paintings — Rich colors and dramatic lighting transfer beautifully
  • Watercolors — Create soft, flowing results with transparent color layers
  • Japanese woodblock prints — Bold outlines and flat color areas

Digital Art & Modern Styles

  • Concept art — Dramatic lighting and fantasy atmospheres
  • Comic book art — Bold lines, strong colors, dynamic compositions
  • Pixel art — Retro gaming aesthetic with blocky pixels
  • Neon/cyberpunk art — Glowing colors against dark backgrounds

Creative Use Cases

Social Media Content

Transform ordinary photos into eye-catching artistic content. A simple coffee shop photo becomes a stunning illustrated post when styled with the right reference.

Gift Ideas

Take a friend's photo and transform it into a "painting." It makes for a unique, personalized digital gift that's easy to print and frame.

Brand Consistency

Create a signature visual style for your brand by using the same reference image across all your photos. This creates a cohesive aesthetic across social media feeds and marketing materials.

Photo Restoration

Old, faded photos can be given new artistic life through style transfer. Rather than trying to restore them to photographic quality, embrace the age by applying a vintage painting style.

Tips for Better Results

  • Match complexity — Simple reference styles work best with simple photos, and detailed reference styles work best with detailed photos
  • Consider color harmony — Choose a reference whose color palette complements your source image
  • Use prompts for control — Add prompts like "preserve face details" or "focus on background transformation" to guide the AI
  • Try multiple references — The same source photo can look dramatically different with different style references
  • Combine with upscaling — After style transfer, upscale the result for maximum quality

Style Transfer vs Image Generation

When should you use style transfer vs generating a new image from scratch?

  • Use style transfer when you have a specific photo you want to keep recognizable but render in a different style
  • Use image generation when you want to create something entirely new from a text description
  • Combine both — Generate an image, then apply style transfer to give it a specific artistic look
