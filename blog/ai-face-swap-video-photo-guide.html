What Is AI Face Swap?

AI face swap is a deep learning technology that replaces one person's face with another in photos or videos while preserving:

  • Original expressions: Smiles, blinks, mouth movements transfer naturally
  • Lighting and shadows: AI matches lighting from target footage
  • Head angle and motion: Face follows original movement
  • Skin tone blending: Edges seamlessly merged

Unlike old photo manipulation, modern AI face swap from 2026 uses neural networks that understand 3D facial geometry, making swaps look like the new person was actually in the original video.

How to Face Swap a Photo

Step 1: Prepare Source and Target

For best results, choose:

  • Source face: Clear, well-lit, front-facing photo
  • Target image: Similar angle and lighting to source
  • Resolution: Both at least 512x512 pixels
  • No obstructions: No hands, hair, glasses covering faces

Step 2: Run the Swap

  1. Open FP AI Studio and tap "Face Swap"
  2. Upload source face photo
  3. Upload target image (where face will go)
  4. Select model: InsightFace for speed, SimSwap for quality
  5. Tap "Generate" - results in 15-30 seconds

Step 3: Refine the Result

  • If skin tone doesn't match: enable color matching
  • If edges visible: increase blend strength
  • If quality degraded: use face restoration
  • If lighting off: try a more similar target image

How to Face Swap a Video

Step 1: Choose Source Photo Wisely

The source photo determines everything:

  • Front-facing portrait: Required for all angles in target video
  • Neutral expression: AI handles emotions from target
  • High quality: 1080p+ source = 1080p+ output
  • Single face only: Crop out other people

Step 2: Select Target Video

Target video best practices:

  • Clear face throughout: No long obstructions
  • Reasonable length: 5-30 seconds for best results
  • Stable shots: Less motion blur = better swap
  • Good lighting: Avoid extreme shadows

Step 3: Generate Video Swap

  1. Upload source face photo
  2. Upload target video (MP4 recommended)
  3. Choose model: Roop Unleashed for long videos, InsightFace for short clips
  4. Enable HD enhancement (optional but recommended)
  5. Tap "Generate" - 30-90 seconds for typical clips
  6. Review and re-run with different settings if needed
