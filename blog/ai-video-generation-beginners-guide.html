What is AI Video Generation?

AI video generation uses artificial intelligence to create videos from text descriptions (prompts) or images. You simply describe what you want to see, and AI brings it to life with realistic motion, physics, and cinematography.

Step 2: Writing Your First Prompt

A prompt is simply a description of the video you want. Here's the beginner-friendly formula:

Basic Prompt Formula:

[Subject] + [Action] + [Setting] + [Style]

Example: "A cat playing piano in a cozy living room, cinematic style"

Beginner Prompt Examples:

  • "A butterfly landing on a flower in slow motion"
  • "Ocean waves crashing on a beach at sunset"
  • "A cup of coffee being poured, steam rising"
  • "Northern lights dancing in the night sky"
  • "A dog running through a field of flowers"

Understanding Video Settings (Beginner-Friendly)

Duration

  • 3-5 seconds: Perfect for beginners, faster generation
  • 10 seconds: Good for simple scenes
  • 15+ seconds: Advanced, takes longer

Aspect Ratios Explained

  • 16:9 - YouTube, TV (horizontal)
  • 9:16 - Instagram Reels, TikTok (vertical)
  • 1:1 - Instagram posts (square)

Motion Amount

  • Low: Subtle movements (good for landscapes)
  • Medium: Balanced (recommended for beginners)
  • High: Dynamic action (can be unpredictable)

Common Beginner Mistakes (And How to Avoid)

Mistake Result Solution
Too complex prompts Confused AI output Start simple, add details gradually
Wrong aspect ratio Cropped videos Choose ratio before generating
Unrealistic expectations Disappointment AI improves with practice
Ignoring style words Generic looking videos Add "cinematic", "realistic", etc.

Prompt Writing Tips for Beginners

Words That Work Magic:

  • For Quality: "cinematic", "high quality", "detailed"
  • For Mood: "dramatic", "peaceful", "energetic"
  • For Lighting: "golden hour", "soft lighting", "sunset"
  • For Camera: "close-up", "wide shot", "aerial view"

Simple Prompt Templates:

  1. Nature: "[Animal/Element] in [Location] during [Time of day]"
  2. Object: "[Object] [Action] with [Effect]"
  3. Scene: "[Setting] with [Atmosphere] and [Lighting]"

Troubleshooting Common Issues

"My video looks weird/distorted"

Solution: Simplify your prompt. Remove conflicting descriptions like "flying underwater".

"Generation is taking forever"

Solution: Use shorter duration (3 seconds) and standard quality for faster results.

"The AI ignored part of my prompt"

Solution: Put important elements first. AI prioritizes early prompt words.

"Motion is too fast/slow"

Solution: Add motion descriptors: "slowly", "quickly", "gradual", "sudden".
