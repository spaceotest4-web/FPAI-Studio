AI Product Photography

Why AI Product Photography?

Professional product photography traditionally requires a studio, lighting equipment, a skilled photographer, and post-production editing. This can cost hundreds to thousands of dollars per shoot. AI changes this equation entirely.

With FP AI Studio, you can generate photorealistic product shots from text descriptions. Whether you're a small business owner, an Etsy seller, or a marketer creating mockups, AI gives you studio-quality results from your phone.

Best Models for Product Photography

Mystic — The Commercial Champion

Mystic excels at clean, commercial-quality images. Its outputs look like they came from a professional stock photo library. For product shots, this is often the ideal choice because the results are clean, well-lit, and market-ready.

Flux 2 Pro — Maximum Detail

When you need the highest resolution and finest details — especially for close-up product shots showing texture, material quality, or intricate design — Flux 2 Pro delivers the most detailed results.

Product Photography Prompt Templates

Clean White Background

"Professional product photo of [your product], centered on pure white background, soft studio lighting, high-end commercial photography, sharp focus, 8K quality"

Lifestyle Context

"[Your product] placed on a marble countertop in a modern kitchen, natural window light, lifestyle photography, warm tones, shallow depth of field"

Flat Lay

"Top-down flat lay of [your product] arranged with complementary items, clean white surface, editorial style, symmetric composition, soft shadows"

Hero Shot

"Dramatic hero shot of [your product] with dark background, single spotlight creating rim lighting, luxury product photography, cinematic feel"

Industry-Specific Tips

Food & Beverage

  • Add "food photography, appetizing, fresh ingredients visible, warm lighting" to your prompts
  • Mention specific plating styles or table settings
  • Use words like "steam rising" or "condensation on glass" for realism

Fashion & Apparel

  • Specify the model's pose, lighting direction, and background
  • Include fabric descriptors: "silk texture visible, soft drape, luxurious sheen"
  • Mention editorial or lookbook style for professional results

Electronics & Tech

  • Use "clean minimal background, gradient lighting, reflective surface"
  • Add "showing the screen at slight angle, tech product photography"
  • Include "subtle reflections on surface" for premium feel

Cosmetics & Beauty

  • Include "dewy texture, rose gold accents, soft feminine lighting"
  • Mention "product splash" or "cream texture close-up" for dynamic shots
  • Use pastel backgrounds and natural botanical elements

Post-Generation Enhancement

After generating your product shot, use FP AI Studio's editing tools to perfect it:

  1. Upscale — Increase resolution to 4x for print-ready quality
  2. Relight — Adjust lighting to match your brand's aesthetic
  3. Expand — Extend the canvas to create more negative space for text overlays

Workflow: From Concept to E-Commerce Ready

  1. Write 3-5 prompt variations for your product
  2. Generate with HyperFlux first (fast iterations)
  3. Refine your best prompt, then generate with Mystic or Flux Pro
  4. Upscale the winner to print quality
  5. Use Expand to create banner/hero versions
  6. Download all variations for your store

This entire workflow takes under 15 minutes and costs a fraction of a traditional photo shoot.
