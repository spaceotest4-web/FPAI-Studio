What Is AI Interior Design?

AI interior design uses computer vision and generative AI to:

  • Analyze your room: Detect walls, windows, doors, ceiling
  • Preserve structure: Keep architectural elements intact
  • Replace furniture: Swap couches, beds, tables with new styles
  • Apply color schemes: Change walls, flooring, accents
  • Add decor: Plants, art, lighting, rugs automatically placed

The 2026 breakthrough: AI now maintains perfect spatial accuracy - sofas fit where they should, lighting matches real physics, and proportions stay realistic.

Room-Specific Tips

Living Rooms

  • Capture from doorway for best perspective
  • Include couch, coffee table area
  • Use "cozy" or "social" keywords
  • Try multiple seating arrangements

Bedrooms

  • Shoot from foot of bed
  • Include nightstands and headboard
  • Try "serene" or "romantic" themes
  • Test different bedding combinations

Kitchens

  • Wide angle showing cabinets and island
  • Try different countertop materials
  • Experiment with backsplash designs
  • Test color schemes (white/dark/two-tone)

Bathrooms

  • Shoot from doorway
  • Include vanity and shower area
  • Try spa-like or luxury themes
  • Test tile pattern variations

Home Offices

  • Show desk and shelving
  • Try "productive" or "creative" themes
  • Test lighting setups
  • Include storage solutions
