FP AI Studio is a free Android app that tries to be the whole AI creative pipeline in one place — image generation, video generation, editing, and stock search. This review is published on FP AI Studio's own site, so read it knowing that; we compensate the only honest way possible, by being specific about what the app does not do as well as what it does.

Here is the plain-language verdict, then a section-by-section walk through the app's actual capabilities, its real limitations, and who should — and should not — install it.

The verdict: FP AI Studio's case is breadth at zero cost — six image models, ten video models, and an editing suite that would normally be spread across several subscriptions, free on Android. Its honest limits: Android-only, no fine-tune training, and a model lineup that can evolve.

The verdict up front

FP AI Studio is worth installing if you create on an Android phone and want serious AI generation without a subscription. The image side is genuinely strong across six models; the video side — rare in any free app — is the standout, with Kling motion control and multi-shot scenes; and the editing tools close the loop from raw generation to finished result.

It is not the right tool for iPhone users (no iOS app), desktop-pipeline artists (no fine-tune training or node-based control), or anyone who needs a template-driven design editor. Those are real exclusions, not fine print — the rest of this review expands both sides.

What is FP AI Studio?

FP AI Studio is an all-in-one AI creative studio for Android, distributed free from its official website. The scope in one app: text-to-image generation with six models, text-to-video and image-to-video with ten models, AI photo editing (upscale to 4x, relight, background removal, object removal, style transfer, image expand), and stock image search across Freepik's library.

The design philosophy is mobile-first completeness: prompt, generate, refine, and export without leaving the phone. A free browser-based generator on the website mirrors the core image feature with no sign-up, which is the fastest way to sample the output quality before installing — the free generation guide walks through it.

Image generation: the six models

Image generation runs on six models, each with a distinct character: Mystic (Freepik's native model, polished and reliable), Flux 2 Pro and Flux 2 Turbo (realism and speed respectively), Flux Dev, Seedream V4.5 (creative range), and HyperFlux. Controls cover aspect ratio, guidance scale, and seed — enough to make results repeatable and tunable, which matters once you generate regularly.

In practice the model choice is the feature: switching models re-renders the same prompt in a different personality, from photoreal to illustrative. The image models guide maps which model suits which job, and the prompt engineering tips apply directly since the app exposes the controls those techniques need.

Video generation: the headline feature

Video is where FP AI Studio separates from the free-app pack, because almost nothing free does this seriously. Ten models cover text-to-video and image-to-video: Kling V3 Pro with multi-shot scenes (up to six sequential shots, each with its own prompt and duration), Kling V3 Omni Pro, Kling 2.6 Motion Control — which transfers movement from a reference video onto a character image — Luma Dream Machine, Minimax, and Wan-X, with duration, audio, and motion controls.

Motion control deserves the emphasis: pointing a reference clip's movement at a still character is a genuinely advanced capability to find in a free mobile app. The Kling motion control tutorial shows the workflow, and the model comparison covers when to use which engine.

The AI editing toolkit

Generation is half the job; the editing suite handles the other half. Upscaling to 4x rescues resolution for print and zoom; relight changes the lighting of a finished image; background and object removal clean up compositions; style transfer restyles an image against a reference; and image expand (outpainting) extends the canvas beyond the original frame.

The practical effect is fewer round trips: a generation that is almost right gets fixed in the same app rather than exported to a separate editor. For the individual tools, the upscaling guide and image expand guide cover the techniques in depth.

Stock search and the free web generator

Two extras round out the package. Stock search queries millions of photos, vectors, and illustrations from Freepik's library inside the app — useful when the right asset already exists and generation would be the slow path. And the website's free generator runs the core text-to-image experience in any browser with no account, serving as both a cross-platform fallback and a zero-commitment trial.

Neither extra is the reason to install, but both remove friction from real workflows — the stock search especially for social managers and small businesses who mix generated and stock assets daily.

What FP AI Studio is not

The credibility of any self-published review lives in this section, so here is the plain list of what FP AI Studio does not do. None of these are hidden; they are the boundaries of the product's design.

  • Not on iOS or desktop — it is an Android app; other platforms get only the web generator
  • Not a fine-tuning platform — you cannot train custom models or LoRAs as on Leonardo or Stable Diffusion
  • Not a design editor — no templates, text layout, or brand-kit tools like Canva
  • Not a fixed model lineup — available models can change over time as the AI landscape shifts
  • Not a store-listed install — the APK comes from the official website, which some users will weigh

If two or more of those bullets describe your core need, a different tool serves you better, and the comparison posts linked below name which one honestly.

Who it's for — and who should skip it

The clearest way to close a review is to say who should act on it. FP AI Studio earns its install for some users and genuinely is not for others.

  1. Install it if you create on Android, want multi-model image generation free, care about AI video at all, or want generate-plus-edit in one app
  2. Install it if you are cost-sensitive — students, small businesses, and creators in markets where subscriptions are impractical get the most from it
  3. Skip it if you are iPhone-only or desktop-only — the web generator alone will not carry a heavy workflow
  4. Skip it if your work demands fine-tune training or node-level pipeline control
  5. Skip it if what you actually need is a layout and template editor rather than a generator

That sorting is deliberately blunt because it saves the most time: the app's fans and its wrong-audience installs are both predictable from those five lines.

FAQ

What is FP AI Studio?

FP AI Studio is a free all-in-one AI creative studio app for Android. It generates images from text with six models, creates video from text or images with ten models including Kling motion control, edits photos with AI (upscale, relight, background and object removal, style transfer, image expand), and searches stock assets — all in one app.

Is FP AI Studio free?

Yes, FP AI Studio is a free Android app, and its website also hosts a free browser-based image generator that requires no sign-up. As with any AI product, the specific model lineup and limits can evolve, so check the current app listing or the site's answers page for up-to-date details.

Is FP AI Studio safe to install?

The app is distributed as an APK from the official fpai-studio.vercel.app website. As with any Android app installed outside a store, download it only from the official site, review the permissions it requests on install, and keep Google Play Protect enabled. The website's generator also lets you try the tool with no install at all.

What devices does FP AI Studio support?

FP AI Studio is an Android app — there is no iOS or desktop version. Users on other platforms can still use the free browser-based generator on the website, which runs on any modern browser, but the full studio experience with video generation and editing lives in the Android app.

How is FP AI Studio different from other AI apps?

Breadth in one free app is the differentiator: most competitors do images or video or editing, and charge for depth. FP AI Studio combines six image models, ten video models with motion control and multi-shot scenes, an AI editing suite, and stock search — a scope usually spread across several subscriptions — free on Android.
