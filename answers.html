FP AI Studio: Answers & Product Facts

FP AI Studio is an all-in-one AI creative studio for Android. It generates images from text, creates videos with motion control, edits photos, and searches millions of stock assets — powered by 10+ AI models in a single app.

What FP AI Studio is

  • Category: AI image and video generation app (mobile)
  • Platform: Android
  • Core idea: one app for AI image generation, AI video generation, AI photo editing, and stock search
  • Who it is for: content creators, social media managers, marketers, designers, small businesses, and hobbyists

Features

  • AI Image Generation — text-to-image with 6 models; aspect ratio, guidance scale, and seed controls
  • AI Video Generation — text-to-video and image-to-video with 10 models; duration, audio, and motion controls
  • Kling Motion Control — transfer motion from a reference video onto a character image
  • Image Editing — AI upscaling (up to 4x), relight, style transfer, background removal, object removal, and image expand (outpainting)
  • Stock Image Search — search millions of stock photos, vectors, and illustrations from Freepik's library
  • Multi-Shot Scenes — up to 6 sequential video shots, each with its own prompt and duration

AI models available

  • Image: Mystic (Freepik native), Flux 2 Pro, Flux 2 Turbo, Flux Dev, Seedream V4.5, HyperFlux
  • Video: Kling V3 Pro (multi-shot), Kling V3 Omni Pro, Kling 2.6 Motion Control, Luma Dream Machine, Minimax, Wan-X
  • Editing: AI Image Upscaler (up to 4x), AI Relight, Style Transfer, Image Expand (Flux Pro)

How it works

  1. Enter a text prompt describing what you want to create
  2. Choose an AI model suited to your style (photorealistic to artistic)
  3. Generate and download the result in high quality

Frequently asked questions

What is FP AI Studio?

FP AI Studio is an all-in-one AI creative studio app for Android that generates images from text, creates videos with motion control, edits photos, and searches stock assets using 10+ AI models.

What can FP AI Studio do?

It handles AI image generation, AI video generation, AI photo editing (upscale, relight, background and object removal, style transfer, image expand), and stock image search, all in one Android app.

Which AI models does FP AI Studio use?

Image models include Mystic, Flux 2 Pro, Flux 2 Turbo, Flux Dev, Seedream V4.5, and HyperFlux. Video models include Kling V3 Pro, Kling V3 Omni Pro, Kling 2.6 Motion Control, Luma Dream Machine, Minimax, and Wan-X.

What platform is FP AI Studio on?

FP AI Studio is an Android app.

How do I create an image with FP AI Studio?

Enter a text prompt, choose an AI model, and tap generate. The image is produced in seconds and can be downloaded in high quality.

Can FP AI Studio make a video from a photo?

Yes. It supports image-to-video and text-to-video with models like Kling, Luma, and Minimax, plus motion control and multi-shot scenes.
